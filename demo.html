ABOUT PLUG MAP

Are you a proud electric vehicle (EV) owner, constantly on the lookout for charging stations during your travels? Look no further! Plug map is a awseome website that shows the ev charging ports throughout your destinations. All you have to do is enter your from and to destination and our website helps you by displaying all the ev charging ports throughout. Accessing Plug map is not limited to your desktop or laptop. The website is optimized for mobile devices, enabling you to find charging stations on the go, right from your smartphone or tablet. Moreover, Plug map offers integration with popular EV navigation systems and mobile apps, ensuring a seamless transition from planning your route to locating the nearest charging station.
